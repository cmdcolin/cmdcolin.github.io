## Your life and work

- More people might be paying attention to you than you think
- Less people might be paying attention to you than you think

## Be ambitious

At this stage, in the AI world, you can solve a lot of technical problems. Do
hard things. Think also about real problems, but think about future problems
also.

Use AI to code. but don't post a thing that you 100% vibe coded and expect
anyone to care

## Be careful what information you let into your brain

New types of "engagement bait" and "rage bait" are now a significant portion of
social media

Many programmers are furiously anti-AI

Many posts these days are AI generated in part or in whole.

Many posts are in bad faith.

Many posts are trying to influence you.

Many posts are weird 'information gathering' posts from AI or companies.

And many are just plain normal posts from normal people that you should still
have a healthy skepticism of
