Things I have done lately

## Quit caffeinated coffee

This was a hard one for me. I think I have spent ears on a terrible
rollercoaster, where I would drink coffee in the morning, it would give me an
upset stomach, and I would crave food to try to quell the pain but it was never
very sufficient and I would spend the day in a half daze.

I would make coffee with cheap coffee in a drip coffee maker, which is likely
pretty acidic. Maybe boutique coffee wouldn't have such a problem but I don't
know if I care really.

This came to a head two weeks ago where I had one of the most stressful days
ever, my own inner turmoil where I had to deliver on multiple projects by end of
day friday

Probably no one even really cared that I delivered on time, but I said I would
do it, so I did it

After this, I felt absolutely burned out

On saturday I said I wouldn't drink coffee, and then I basically slept for
literally 24 hours straight. I spent the entire day in bed, taking 5+ long naps,
and then slept soundly through the night still.
