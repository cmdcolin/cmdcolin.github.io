This is a crazy probably repetitive region of the bovine genome. The AGP track shows assembly gaps [1], and has red tick marks showing gaps of unknown size. The FM.01.sorted.bam track is a mate pair sequencing dataset, which means that it is composed of many different pieces of DNA that are "paired" together, connected by arcs here. The strange gaps and repetitions cause the "rainbow effects". Normally the reads would be evenly spaced out (and have a consistent color) the gaps of unknown size on the assembly force artificially larger or smaller gaps between paired reads. Large vertical lines show reads that are paired to regions on totally different parts of the chromosome (probably another repetitive region)
